Your cleaning checklist — quick guide 🧹✨

App v5.5 · April 2026

📲 Save the app to your phone first

📲

Add it to your home screen 🏠

The checklist works in your phone browser — but you can save it to your home screen so it opens like an app, even without internet.

1

Open the link using Google Chrome

Make sure you are using Google Chrome — not the Facebook browser or any other app. Tap the link Lloyd shared to open it.

2

Tap the 3 dots at the top right ⋮

Look for the three vertical dots in the top-right corner of Chrome. Tap them.

3

Tap "Add to Home screen"

Scroll down the menu a little if you don't see it. Tap it once.

4

Tap "Add" — and you're done! 🎉

A Cascade Hideaway icon will appear on your home screen. Next time, just tap that icon to open the app — no need to open Chrome first.

✅

Works even when there's no signal!

Once saved, the app opens instantly from your home screen — even inside the condo with no internet. Your report is saved and sent automatically when you reconnect.

🚀 Here's how it works — step by step

1
Required · takes 1 min

👋 Enter your name and the date

Tap your name from the list. If your name is not there, tap "Type name manually" and type it.

Check that the date and start time are correct. The Next button stays grey until you enter your name — this is normal.

2
💬 Facebook Group ChatRequired

📸 Send before-photos to the group chat first

Do this before you start cleaning! Tap the blue "Open Group Chat" button. The app automatically copies a ready-made message for you.

📸 PRE-CLEAN PHOTOS 📅 Saturday, April 19, 2026 🧹 Cleaner: Jary Mabandos 📍 Cascade Hideaway [Photos attached — see areas in checklist]

In Messenger: paste the copied text, then attach your room photos (living area, bedroom, bathroom, kitchen) and send. These photos protect you and the host!

3
Required

⚡💧 Record the meter readings + take photos

Still on the Pre-Clean tab. Enter the electric meter reading (kWh) and the water meter reading (m³). The app will show you the previous readings automatically.

💧

💧 Water meter — always add the decimal point!

The last 3 digits on the water meter are after the decimal. For example: if the meter shows 0003 7 | 2 8 8, type 37.288 — not 37288. The app will warn you if you forget.

After entering the readings, take exactly 2 photos — one photo of the electric meter and one of the water meter. All digits must be sharp and readable.

4
Go through each tab

🧹 Check items off as you clean

Swipe left or tap the tabs at the bottom to move between sections:

Arrive → Chem → Room → Bath → Final

Tick each item as you finish it. The progress bar fills up as you go — try to hit 100%! 🎯 Found a problem? Tap the "⚠️ Found a Problem Here?" button to upload a photo and leave a note all in one go.

5
Can't skip these! 🔒

🔴 Some items need a tap — not just a checkbox

For critical items, you must tap one of the two buttons below. You can't submit without answering all of them:

🗝️ Verify Key Card Is in Its Designated Spot on Keychain
✅ All Good
⚠️ Issue Found

Tap Issue Found? A text box appears — describe the problem and take a photo. The host will be notified right away.

6
🗓️ Weekends only

🔧 Weekend? There's a bonus tab for you!

On Saturdays and Sundays, an extra Weekly tab shows up between Final and Submit. It has deeper cleaning tasks like washing AC filters, cleaning the fridge inside, clearing drains, and checking all light bulbs. Finish those too before submitting!

7
Required

✅ Tap Submit — you're almost done!

On the last tab, tap "✅ Submit Report". The app sends everything to the host automatically — the spreadsheet, an email, and a calendar entry — all in one go. 🙌

📵 No connection — your data is safe. Will retry when back online.
🔄 Back online — retrying your submission…

If you see the red message — don't close the app! Just move closer to a window or step outside. The app will send your report automatically as soon as it catches signal. 📶

8
💬 Facebook Group ChatRequired

💬 Send your after-photos to the group chat

After submitting, a success screen pops up! Tap "💬 Copy Report & Send After Photos" — this copies a neat summary and opens Messenger automatically.

In Messenger: paste the text, attach your after-cleaning photos (living area, bedroom, bathroom, kitchen), and send.

Before-photos at the start + after-photos at the end = the full cleaning record. 📸✅

9
Bonus step 🌟

🧴 Log which supplies you used

On the success screen, tap "Open Inventory" and log how much of each supply you used — soap, shampoo, coffee packs, water bottles, etc. This helps the host restock on time so you never run out mid-cleaning!

💡 One handy tip

📋

Switch between Detail mode and Quick mode

There is a small button in the bottom bar of the app. Tap it to switch between Detail (shows all instructions — good when you are still learning) and Quick (hides the instructions — faster once you know the steps).

📡 Lost signal? Don't worry!

📡

Your data is always safe, promise! 🛡️

The app saves your entire report as you fill it out. Even if you submit with no signal, it holds the data and sends it automatically when your connection comes back. Do not close the app — just wait for the green message.
